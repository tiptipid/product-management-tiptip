SatuSatu Product Playbook

The Operating System for Our Product Team

Moving from insight to impact, together.

Welcome everyone. Today we're introducing the SatuSatu Product Playbook. This isn't a mandate; it's a living operating system for how we prioritize, build, and measure output.

Why Now: The Opportunity

Our Goal: Parity first, differentiate second. Fix the leaky funnel for foreign visitors.
  • The Reality: We have a highly differentiated value proposition (authentic experiences).
  • The Gap: We average 4.1/10 vs competitors' 8.0 in UX benchmarks.
xychart-beta title "Benchmark: SatuSatu vs. Competitor (1-10)" x-axis ["Catalog", "Search", "Book", "Trust", "Loc", "Mob", "Soc", "Price"] y-axis "Score" 0 --> 10 bar [4, 4, 5, 4, 2, 6, 3, 5] line [8.5, 8.0, 7.75, 7.75, 8.25, 7.75, 7.75, 8.0]
We have authentic experiences competitors lack, but we lose foreign visitors to UX friction (see the chart, the line is competitors, the bars are us). We must fix this.

What stays the same?

This playbook layers on top of our culture, it doesn't replace it.

  • Agile Rituals: Sprints, standups, and retrospectives remain intact.
  • Jira & Confluence: Still our home for execution and docs.
  • Squad Autonomy: Teams still own *how* to solve problems.
We're not throwing out what works. Jira is still where we execute. The Playbook just governs how an idea gets into Jira.

What's Changing: The Framework

Three core workflow upgrades:

  1. 4-Layer Prioritization System
  2. AI-Assisted Workflow
  3. Unified Backlog Tracker
